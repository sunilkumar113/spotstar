Name Sabari
Plot Sabari is a 2024 Indian Telugu-language action thriller film written and directed by Anilkatz in his directorial debut and produced by Mahendra Nath Kondla. The film stars Varalaxmi Sarathkumar in the titular role alongside Mime Gopi, Ganesh Venkatraman, and Shashank.
