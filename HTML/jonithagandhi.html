JONITHA GANDHI

About :

Jonita Gandhi is a versatile Indo-Canadian playback singer known for her soulful renditions in Bollywood and South Indian film industries. Her journey into music began with YouTube covers, eventually leading to collaborations with renowned composers. Jonita's distinctive voice and emotive delivery have earned her acclaim and a loyal fan base. With a repertoire spanning various genres, she continues to captivate audiences with her vocal prowess and musical versatility. Jonita remains a rising star, contributing her talent to numerous chart-topping soundtracks.
