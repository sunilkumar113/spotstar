Name Chow Chow Bath
Plot Chow Chow Bath is a 2024 Indian Kannada-language romantic comedy film written and directed by Kenja Chethan Kumar. It is Kannada's first Hyperlink Rom-Com. Hyperlink cinema is one of the very rarely used narrative structures in the industry.
Genre Comedy/Romance
Release date 15 March 2024
