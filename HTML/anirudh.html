ANIRUDH RAVICHANDEER

About :

Anirudh Ravichander is a prominent Indian music composer and singer predominantly working in the Tamil film industry. He gained widespread recognition with his debut soundtrack for the film "3," which included the viral hit "Why This Kolaveri Di." Anirudh is known for his catchy tunes, youthful energy, and experimentation with different musical styles. His innovative compositions have made him a favorite among the younger generation of filmgoers. With numerous chart-topping hits to his name, Anirudh continues to push the boundaries of Tamil film music.
