Name For Regn
Plot For Regn is a 2024 Indian Kannada-language romantic drama film written and directed by Naveen Dwarakanath, in his debut and produced by Naveen Rao. The film features Pruthvi Ambaar and Milana Nagaraj in the lead roles. The film's score is by Nakul Abhyankar and soundtrack is composed by R. K. Harish.
