Name Mathimaran
Plot Mathimaran is a 2023 Indian Tamil-language family drama and crime thriller film written and directed by Mantra Veerapandian and produced by Lenin Babu. It stars Venkat Senguttuvan and Ivana as the titular characters and Aradhya with M. S. Bhaskar in a supporting role.
