G.V. PRAKASH

About :

G.V. Prakash Kumar, also known as GVP, is a multifaceted talent in the Indian film industry, excelling as a music composer, actor, and singer primarily in Tamil cinema. He comes from a family deeply rooted in the music industry, with renowned composer A.R. Rahman as his uncle. GVP's music is characterized by its youthful charm and catchy melodies, earning him a dedicated following among fans. Apart from his musical prowess, he has also showcased his acting skills in several successful films, further cementing his place in the industry. With a diverse portfolio and continued success, G.V. Prakash Kumar remains a prominent figure in Tamil entertainment.
